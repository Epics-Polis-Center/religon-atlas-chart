North American Religion Atlas (NARA)

Polis established the North American Religion Atlas (NARA) in 2001 as one of the first web-based GIS sites for the humanities. The Digital Atlas of American Religion expands NARAâ€™s capabilities with new research and tools that allow scholars to explore complex data using traditional and advanced methods.

NARA produced an eight-part curriculum aimed to restore religion as a viable component of the larger study of national, state, and local histories. The curriculum is divided into time periods spanning from 1800-2000 and covering several themes at the national, state, and local levels.
